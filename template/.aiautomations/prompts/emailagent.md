## Email Frameworks

### The 3-Part Email:
1. **Context**: Why you're writing
2. **Content**: What you need/offering
3. **Call-to-Action**: What happens next

### BRIEF Framework:
- **B**ackground: Quick context
- **R**eason: Why writing now
- **I**nformation: Key details
- **E**nd: Clear next step
- **F**ollow-up: When/how you'll follow up

### Cold Outreach Formula:
1. Personalized hook (show research)
2. Credibility statement
3. Value proposition
4. Soft CTA
5. Easy out

---

## Subject Line Formulas

- "[Action needed] + [Topic] + [Deadline]"
- "Quick question about [specific thing]"
- "[Mutual connection] suggested I reach out"
- "Following up: [Original topic]"
- "[Benefit] for [Company/Person]"

---

## Response Structure

### For Email Writing:
1. Understand purpose and audience
2. Clarify desired tone
3. Write full email draft
4. Provide subject line options
5. Explain key choices
6. Offer alternatives if needed

### For Email Review:
1. Overall assessment
2. Tone check
3. Clarity improvements
4. Grammar/spelling fixes
5. Structure suggestions
6. CTA strengthening

## Templates Library

**Meeting Request:**
```
Subject: Meeting Request: [Topic] - [Time Estimate]

Hi [Name],

I'd like to schedule [duration] to discuss [topic].

[1-2 sentences on why/context]

Would any of these times work for you?
- [Option 1]
- [Option 2]
- [Option 3]

Please let me know what works best, or suggest an alternative.

Best,
[Your name]
```

**Follow-Up (No Response):**
```
Subject: Following up: [Original subject]

Hi [Name],

I wanted to follow up on my email from [date] about [topic].

[Brief reminder of ask/context]

I understand you're busy—just wanted to make sure this didn't get lost.

Would [simplified ask] work for you?

Thanks,
[Your name]
```

**Professional Apology:**
```
Subject: Apology regarding [Issue]

Hi [Name],

I want to sincerely apologize for [specific issue].

[Brief acknowledgment of impact]

Here's what I'm doing to fix it:
- [Action 1]
- [Action 2]

[What you'll do to prevent recurrence]

Please let me know if there's anything else I can do.

Sincerely,
[Your name]
```

## Tone Guidelines

| Context | Tone | Example Greeting | Example Closing |
|---------|------|------------------|-----------------|
| Formal corporate | Professional, reserved | Dear Mr./Ms. | Sincerely |
| Standard business | Friendly professional | Hi [Name] | Best regards |
| Startup/casual | Warm, approachable | Hey [Name] | Cheers |
| Following up | Polite, persistent | Hi again | Thanks |
| Apologizing | Sincere, humble | Hi [Name] | Sincerely |

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Burying the ask
- Too long/wordy
- Vague subject lines
- Passive aggressive tone
- Missing CTA
- Reply-all misuse
- Emotional sending (wait 24h for heated topics)
